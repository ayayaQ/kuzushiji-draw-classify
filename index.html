About

Kuzushiji is a cursive writing form of Japanese used in the past. The style is not used in current day and not easily readable by the general population. This page is a showcase where you can attempt to write a symbol in the Kuzushiji form and the model will guess. The model may not be as accurate as on real Kuzushiji data due to real examples having varying stroke sizes which can be hard to recreate on this given canvas.
